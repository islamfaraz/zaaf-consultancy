// 03 — expertise

Deep capability areas

Seven focused domains. Each one built on real delivery experience, not sales literature.

DevOps & CI/CD

The real challenge isn't automation — it's culture.

Most teams already know they need CI/CD. The blocker is not tooling — it is the absence of agreed standards, ownership, and a pipeline that engineers actually trust. We build pipelines that teams adopt willingly.

  • Pipeline design and migration across GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, Jenkins, and ArgoCD
  • GitOps workflows with policy-as-code gates and automated rollback strategies
  • DORA metric baselines, deployment frequency tracking, and team enablement

Case study

A global logistics provider reduced mean deployment time from 4 hours to 14 minutes. Zero rollbacks in the following six months.

Infrastructure as Code

Click-ops is a liability, not a workflow.

Manually provisioned infrastructure is unauditable, unrepeatable, and expensive to maintain. We migrate teams to fully codified infrastructure with state management, module libraries, and automated drift detection from day one.

  • Full migration from console-managed to IaC-managed environments
  • Custom Terraform module libraries, state backends, and governance patterns
  • CI-integrated drift detection and policy enforcement with Checkov and OPA

Case study

A FinTech Series B achieved 100% IaC coverage across three AWS accounts in 12 weeks. Next environment provisioned in under two hours without ZAAF involvement.

Cloud Architecture

Cloud is not a destination — it's an operating model.

Lifting and shifting on-premise workloads to the cloud rarely solves the underlying problem. We design cloud environments around your actual usage patterns, team capabilities, and cost tolerance.

  • Multi-cloud and hybrid cloud architecture across AWS, Azure, and GCP
  • Cost optimisation, reserved capacity planning, and FinOps dashboards
  • Landing zone design and network segmentation for regulated industries

Case study

An NHS Trust reduced cloud spend by 38% within 90 days following a structured FinOps review and workload right-sizing exercise. No service degradation.

Managed IT Services

Support should be proactive, not reactive.

Generic managed service providers respond to tickets. We monitor infrastructure proactively, identify degradation before it becomes an incident, and maintain a working knowledge of your system's history. You get a named engineer, not a rota.

  • Defined SLA tiers with transparent escalation paths and engineer-to-client ratios
  • Observability stack: Prometheus, Grafana, PagerDuty, and custom alerting logic
  • Monthly service reviews with written findings, not just uptime dashboards

Case study

A B2B SaaS company moved to ZAAF retained support. Incident volume dropped 61% and average resolution time fell from 6 hours to 47 minutes in year one.

Web & App Development

We build digital products with technology your engineers can maintain. We make deliberate stack choices, document architectural decisions as we go, and treat the handover as a deliverable in its own right.

// 04 — how we work

From idea to live product,
without the guesswork.

Every engagement follows the same five phases. Predictable, transparent, and built around one goal — a system your team can own the day we leave.

  1. 01

    Discovery

    We spend the first week listening, not presenting. Structured interviews across engineering, operations, and leadership surface the real problem — not the assumed one. Most teams are surprised by what emerges.

    • Stakeholder interviews across all functions
    • Current-state technical assessment
    • Problem statement document, signed off
    • Risk register and dependency map
    • Engagement scope and success criteria
    • Written findings shared with your team

    Typical duration: 1 week

  2. 02

    Architecture & Design

    A written architecture document before a single line of code. Every decision documented, every alternative considered, every trade-off made explicit. You review it, we revise it, then we build — in that order.

    • System architecture document
    • Infrastructure and data flow diagrams
    • Technology stack decision record
    • Security and compliance design review
    • UI/UX wireframes and component design
    • Sprint plan with milestones and owners

    Typical duration: 1–2 weeks

  3. 03

    Build & Deliver

    Two-week sprints with written progress updates and no surprises. Your engineers pair with ours throughout — not as observers, but as active participants. Knowledge transfer is not an add-on at the end; it is baked into every sprint.

    • Bi-weekly sprint demos with your team
    • Automated test suite built alongside code
    • CI/CD pipeline configured from day one
    • Code review sessions for knowledge transfer
    • Staging environment with real data flows
    • Weekly written status updates

    Typical duration: 4–12 weeks

  4. 04

    Launch & Handover

    A formal handover sprint — not a 90-minute call with a shared folder. Full documentation, runbooks, architecture diagrams, and a structured knowledge transfer programme. We define success as your team being able to operate the system without us.

    • Production deployment with zero-downtime plan
    • Runbooks for every operational scenario
    • Architecture and API documentation
    • Monitoring, alerting, and on-call setup
    • Structured knowledge transfer sessions
    • 30-day hypercare support window

    Typical duration: 1–2 weeks

  5. 05

    Ongoing Support

    Optional

    For organisations that want ongoing engineering capability, we offer a retained arrangement: a named engineer, defined monthly capacity, monthly service review, and a direct line — not a ticket portal. Scope is agreed quarterly with a clear exit clause.

    • Named engineer with context of your system
    • Defined hours per month, no ambiguity
    • Monthly service review with written findings
    • Priority response SLAs for incidents
    • Quarterly roadmap and scope review
    • Clear exit criteria — no lock-in

    Rolling monthly engagement
