Philosophy

Most IT consultancies sell effort. We sell outcomes with defined exit criteria. Our engagements are scoped, time-boxed, and designed to leave your team more capable than when we arrived.

ZAAF was built in response to a pattern we kept seeing: organisations paying for years of consultancy dependency while their internal capability quietly atrophied. We break that cycle.

We work with engineering teams inside growth-stage companies, public sector organisations, and enterprises undergoing infrastructure modernisation. If you can articulate the problem, we can engineer the solution.
